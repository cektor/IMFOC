This Privacy Policy describes how IMFOC ("we", "our", or "the app") handles information when you use our mobile application. IMFOC is an image format converter application developed by ALG Yazılım Inc.

### 2. Information We Collect

**IMFOC DOES NOT COLLECT, STORE, OR TRANSMIT ANY PERSONAL DATA.**

The app operates entirely offline and all image processing is performed locally on your device. We do not have access to any of your data.

### 3. Permissions Used

The app requires the following permissions to function:

#### 3.1 Storage Permissions

**READ_MEDIA_IMAGES (Android 13+)**
- **Purpose:** To allow you to select images from your device gallery for conversion
- **Usage:** Only activated when you tap the "Select Image" button
- **Scope:** Read-only access to images you explicitly select
- **Data Storage:** No data is stored or transmitted

**READ_EXTERNAL_STORAGE (Android 12 and below)**
- **Purpose:** To allow you to select images from your device storage for conversion
- **Usage:** Only activated when you tap the "Select Image" button
- **Scope:** Read-only access to images you explicitly select
- **Data Storage:** No data is stored or transmitted

**WRITE_EXTERNAL_STORAGE (Android 9 and below)**
- **Purpose:** To save converted images to your device
- **Usage:** Only activated when you convert an image
- **Scope:** Write access only to the Pictures/IMFOC folder
- **Data Storage:** Converted images are saved locally on your device only

### 4. How We Use Information

- **Image Processing:** All image conversion and resizing operations are performed locally on your device
- **File Storage:** Converted images are saved to your device's Pictures/IMFOC folder
- **No Cloud Storage:** No images or data are uploaded to any server or cloud service
- **No Analytics:** We do not use any analytics or tracking services
- **No Third-Party Services:** The app does not integrate with any third-party services

### 5. Data Sharing

**WE DO NOT SHARE ANY DATA** because we do not collect any data. All operations are performed locally on your device.

### 6. Data Security

- All image processing occurs locally on your device
- No internet connection is required or used
- No data is transmitted outside your device
- You have complete control over your images and converted files

### 7. Children's Privacy

IMFOC does not collect any personal information from anyone, including children under 13. The app is safe for users of all ages.

### 8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date. We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically.

### 9. Your Rights

Since we do not collect any personal data, there is no data to access, modify, or delete. You have complete control over:
- Images you select for conversion
- Converted images saved on your device
- App permissions through your device settings

### 10. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us:

- **Developer Email:** fatih@algyazilim.com
- **Website:** www.algyazilim.com
- **GitHub:** https://github.com/cektor/IMFOC

### 11. Compliance

This app complies with:
